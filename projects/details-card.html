Summary

The Interactive Card is a React application I built so I could show direct value to companies - in the sense that I could provide a user friendly and visually appealing form.

Challenges

The first challenge I ran into was how to approach the spacing on the credit card. After researching a few approaches, I decided that using the &nbsp entity to create space in the numbers.

The other challenging part was of course setting up all the validations and making sure they all worked properly. For the challenge, I didn't have to include very many, but I wanted to get a more accurate experience.
